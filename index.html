About

I'm a software engineering leader with experience in launching innovative products at Riot Games, Twitch, and Microsoft. I enjoy shipping in fast-paced and ambiguous environments, building technology at scale, growing people and teams, and serving in gaming and creator product spaces.

Currently, I lead the content and metagame systems initiative on an unannounced title at Riot Games. Previously, I built matchmaking, stats, progression, and competitive systems for Halo. I also worked at Twitch on eSports, Subscriptions, Bits, and Emotes.

My personal mission is to support women and underrepresented minorities in tech. I co-founded a Women in Engineering Group at Twitch. I mentored with the Rainier Scholars, Women in STEM Mentorship Program, and University of Washington. I was a speaker at the Rainier Scholars Career Expo and Twitch's Women in Tech Panel.

In my free time, I stream on Twitch and am a local food blogger on Instagram.
